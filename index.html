Junior colleges can start the form filling process of Class 12 students from October 1 informed the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) on Thursday.

The examination of Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or Class 12 is scheduled in February and March 2023. The forms are to be filled by online mode through the SARAL database.
